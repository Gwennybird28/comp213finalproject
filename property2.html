Luxurious House

Welcome to "SkySummit" where every room is an experience. Perched on nearly an acre in The Summit Club. SkySummit defines the word majestic. This modern, open concept design truly embodies and captures the best of desert living. With its floor-to-ceiling windows, pocket doors, private 'zen' courtyard, extensive shaded patios, pool deck, and balcony, it truly is a resort unto itself. With smooth white stucco exterior and stone columns, the entrance ushers you into a grand foyer. A great room holds a seating area ringed by beautiful stone, and first and second-story windows allow gorgeous mountain views. The dining area and kitchen, complete with granite island with bar seating, adjoin. A first-level primary suite boasts mountain views, two custom walk-in closets, and an indoor/outdoor master bath ringed by chic landscaping.A separate set of stairs leads to a second level that includes an additional living area with a kitchen and dining area, a private balcony, and 2 bedrooms.
